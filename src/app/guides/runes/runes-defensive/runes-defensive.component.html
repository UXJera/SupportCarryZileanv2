Defensive Runes

This page is used for surviving the early game and transitioning into a utility-based tank who absorbs damage for your carries

Rune Page Highlights

Guardian

Guardian is a simple keystone that provides a small shield to both you and your closest ally if you take damage. This also can be triggered when you speed boost an ally at low health. Many opponents underestimate the shield absorption and fail to execute on an all-in.

Font of Life

Font of Life is the most practical of the three choices, and also provides an outlet for Ardent Censer (similar to Stoneborn Pact / Ardent Censer from Season 7).

Iron Skin or Mirror Shell

The choice of these runes should be decided by what you are laning against. Facing lots of AD threats? Take extra armor. Facing a poke lane (Karma/Sona)? Take the extra MR. Don't forget you get bonus stats when using a potion.

Manaflow Band

Manaflow Band is incredible because it gives a free spell and returns a percentage of your empty mana every minute. This is crucial in lane where you don't have mana or mana regen items.

Transcendence

Transcendence is an all-star because it gives a free 10% CDR, and turns extra CDR into AP. This is great because it accelerates the pace toward 40% CDR, and adds value for exceeding the CDR cap of 40%.
