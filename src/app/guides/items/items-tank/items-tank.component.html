Tank Item Build Overview

Recommended Runes

The tank build is an off-meta approach to playing Zilean. Its job is to play closer to the front-line looking for engages, then quickly retreating to protect the back line.

This build is fun because it is incredibly difficult to kill the Zilean and most people end up overchasing.

Starting Items

Coin is the best option because you will be playing reactively in lane, meaning that you will not be the aggressor. Your goal is to survive lane and get enough of an income to buy defensive items.

Early Game Items

Upgrade your Coin and then buy Sightsotne, after that build a Glacial Shroud for the extra mana and CDR. This should help you take a few hits from the enemy ADC.

Mid Game Core Items

Righteous Glory is a great item for creating a pick, and most people don't expect Zilean speeding in at over 700 MS with a plethora of slows. Knight's Vow is a great defensive item to protect a carry, and also gives you CDR and life to survive even longer.

End Game Items (Full Tank)

Wrapping the build out with a Locket of the Iron Solari is a great idea because you have a decent amount of extra health to make the shield larger. These tanky items give you a much higher chance to escape from a fight, and encourage you to use your ultimate on carries rather than yourself.
