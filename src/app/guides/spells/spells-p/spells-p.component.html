Passive: Heightened Learning

Range: 1000

Spell Description: Zilean generates experience (based on level) every 5 seconds, which does not advance his own level.

When Zilean has enough to level up an allied champion and is out of combat for 10 seconds, you can right-click them for a 2 second channel, after which he grants them enough experience to finish their level and Zilean receives the same amount.

A small circle beneath allied champions indicates how close they are to the next level. If the circle is full, you can grant that champion an extra level.

If you interrupt the channel, Time in a Bottle goes on cooldown for 2 seconds. If you are interrupted by an enemy champion, Time in a Bottle goes on cooldown for 10 seconds.

Time in the Bottle can be active at 2 minutes and starts with 48 XP.

Early Game Usage

Most of the time, you'll want to save the passive for the level 2 -> 3 transition. The reason is that it opens up your full kit with the slow.

Do not use the passive to level from 1 -> 2. The xp gained at that time is equivalent to 1 ranged minion, plus the cooldown makes you miss the level 2 -> 3 level gain.

Prioritizing Passive in the Mid-Game

Generally, you want to use this on someone who can get another rank in an ultimate (11,16) and also on your jungler to have a stronger smite.

After that, you can either give XP to the highest level player (if you are ahead) or to your lowest level player (if you are behind).
