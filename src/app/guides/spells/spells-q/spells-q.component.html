Q - Time Bomb

Spell Description: Zilean throws a ticking time bomb to the target location that detonates after 3 seconds, dealing magic damage to all surrounding enemies.

If a unit is near a Time Bomb as it lands or has already landed, they pick it up and become the holder, causing it to detonate at their location at the end of its duration.

If a bomb holder picks up another Time Bomb, the first bomb immediately detonates and briefly stuns all affected enemies.

Range:
900 (skillshot)
300 (blast radius)
100 (bomb attach radius)

Cast Delay: .25 second animation of casting

Travel Delay: .5 seconds at max range

Bombs attach to targets in order of priority:

  • Any Unit Carrying a Bomb
  • Enemy Champions
  • Allied Champions
  • Enemy Minions
  • Allied Minions

Time Bomb

Time Bomb is a versatile spell that can deal damage, stun multiple targets, or zone areas. Even a single Time Bomb can deliver a considerable amount of damage in the early game.

There are several applications that you can use Double Bomb with that may seem unintuitive:

Double Bomb onto Minions to Trigger Stun on Champion
Double Bomb onto Allies to Trigger Stun
Self-Double Bomb to Stun Enemies On You

You can do a fancy play by throwing the Bomb on yourself, tossing the second bomb, and flashing into it to detonate.

Triple Bomb / Chain Stun w/ Buffering

This is one of the hardest tricks to use effectively. The gist is that you have enough CDR and spell ranks to keep Rewind on 100% cooldown while holding the detonation throw of Time Bomb until the end of the Time Bomb on the Target. This video goes more in-depth about how exactly it works.

Mana Conservation

The best strategy for conserving mana is to not throw Double Bombs unless you are guaranteed a kill or disengaging. Using Single Bombs to poke is can give you lane pressure.

And remember, if you are doing to Double Bomb, always try to land a slow first so you have a better chance of landing the stun.
